Why We Built EasyStay

Hospitality businesses today are forced to juggle multiple disconnected tools — one for bookings, another for billing, a separate POS for the restaurant, spreadsheets for expenses, and yet another platform for their website. This fragmentation leads to inefficiency, data silos, and a poor experience for both staff and guests.

Our Mission

EasyStay was born from the real-world operations of Ganesh Residency, a premium beachfront resort in Pondicherry. We experienced firsthand the pain of managing a hospitality business across disconnected systems. So we built the solution we wished existed — a single, elegant platform that handles everything from the moment a guest discovers your property to the final financial report.

The Problems We Solve

Fragmented Operations

No more switching between 5+ tools. One platform for bookings, rooms, restaurant, invoices, and your website.

High OTA Commissions

Take back control with direct bookings through your own branded website — save 15-25% on every reservation.

Manual Financial Tracking

Automated GST-compliant invoicing, expense tracking, and real-time profit & loss reporting.

No Tech Expertise Needed

A beautiful, customizable website for your property with zero coding. Edit content, galleries, and rooms in minutes.
